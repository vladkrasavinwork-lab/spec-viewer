# AI-assisted development

Model AI applicability per work item. Consider code/test generation, documentation, code-review
support, architecture support, human review, context preparation, evaluation, and likely rework.

High applicability often includes boilerplate, conventional CRUD, typed clients, test scaffolding,
documentation, and bounded refactoring. Low applicability often includes unclear business rules,
architecture trade-offs, threat modeling, compliance approval, unstable integrations, migrations,
and production incidents.

Do not use one project-wide reduction percentage. An AI-assisted item may equal or exceed baseline
effort when review and rework dominate. Never assume autonomous production changes.
