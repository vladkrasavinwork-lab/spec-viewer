# Requirement ID policy

Requirement IDs use stable uppercase forms such as `REQ-AUTH-001`, `REQ-PAY-004`,
`REQ-DATA-012`, `NFR-SEC-003`, and `NFR-PERF-002`. IDs are unique within a project, remain stable
during editorial changes, and are never reassigned after removal. New requirements receive the next
available number in their domain; renumbering for presentation is prohibited.
